About VetVax Planner

Bridging the gap between veterinary medicine and data-driven pet care.

Our Mission

To provide pet owners and veterinarians with a reliable, accurate, and easy-to-use platform for managing vaccination schedules, ensuring every pet receives the core protection they need to live a healthy life.

Expert Foundations

Our logic is built upon the 2026 Stiko Vet requirements from Germany, reflecting modern veterinary standards and protocols for companion animals.

Personalized Care

Every pet is unique. Our planner takes into account species, lifestyle, and travel history to recommend the optimal vaccination series.

Core Principles

  • Proactive Prevention: Anticipating medical needs before they become emergencies.
  • Data Transparency: Providing owners with clear visibility into their pets' medical timelines.
  • Professional Collaboration: Supporting the vital relationship between pets, owners, and their veterinarians.

VetVax Planner is an educational project developed for Django demonstration purposes.
